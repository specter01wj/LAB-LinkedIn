Dr. Winthrop

Dr. Winthrop is the guardian of Missy, a three-year old Llaso mix, who he adopted at the shelter. Dr. Winthrop is passionate about spay and neuter and pet adoption, and works tireless hours outside the clinic, performing free spay and neuter surgeries for the shelter.

Doctor's Tip: Regular checkups are a key factor in pet wellness, and can often unearth problems that could lead to health issues down the road. The American Animal Hospital Association recommends annual checkups for cats and dogs, and checkups every six months for senior cats and dogs. Annual checkups are also recommended for birds, rabbits, ferrets, reptiles, rodents, and other exotic pets.
